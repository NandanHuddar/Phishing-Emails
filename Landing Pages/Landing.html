How to Spot Phishing Emails

Your guide to protecting yourself from email scams

Key Warning Signs

  • Check sender's email address for misspellings
  • Be cautious of urgent or threatening language
  • Look for poor grammar and spelling mistakes
  • Avoid clicking unexpected attachments
  • Hover over links to preview URLs

Common Phishing Tactics

Fake Urgency: Creating panic to force quick, careless actions
Impersonation: Pretending to be legitimate companies
Too Good to Be True: Promising unrealistic rewards

What to Do If You Suspect a Phishing Email

1 Don't click any links or download attachments
2 Report the email to your IT department
3 Delete the email from your inbox
4 If you clicked anything, change passwords immediately

Stay vigilant and protect yourself from phishing attempts!
