What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cybercrime where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations or individuals to steal sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, and personal data. At EduSafe.ie, our mission is to educate and empower individuals to stay safe online.

How to Prevent Phishing Scams

Verify Links

Always hover over links to check their destination before clicking. Look out for misspelled URLs or suspicious domains.

Check Sender Details

Be cautious of emails from unknown senders or addresses that don't match the organization they claim to represent.

Avoid Sharing Personal Info

Never share sensitive information, like passwords or PINs, via email or unverified websites.
